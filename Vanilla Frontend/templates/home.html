What Are Exoplanets?

Exoplanets are planets that orbit stars outside our solar system. Some may even be habitable, making them the focus of scientific research.

Discover the fascinating characteristics of these distant worlds and how they are detected through advanced space missions.

What is ExoExplorer?

ExoExplorer is an interactive platform that allows students and enthusiasts to learn about exoplanets through visualizations, data insights, and fun activities.

Our mission is to spark curiosity about space exploration and provide accessible learning tools for everyone.
