Brief Overview

Exoplanets are planets that exist beyond our solar system. While most of them orbit other stars, some roam freely in space without a star to call home. We’ve discovered over 5,600 exoplanets so far, but scientists believe that billions more are waiting to be found. These planets vary greatly in size, composition, and orbit.
